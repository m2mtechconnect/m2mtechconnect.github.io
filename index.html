About our Offering

Couple of years ago, when we first started working on this initiative - never thought we would receive such a tremendous response from the youths. Today as we speak, we have completed 400+ hands-on STEM workshops focussed on Coding and Design Thinking empowering 500+ participants, 4300+ hours of Coding & Design Thinking.

Youths have actively participated in our STEM workshops covering topics like Animation, Gaming, Graphics, 3D Printing, Virtual Reality, Digital Marketing, Website development, Python, Data Visualization, Data Analysis, Machine Learning, Digital Logic, Embedded System and more. Our STEM workshops have given students pointers to become creators to create and communicate their own STEM projects which is evident from the active participation in our TECH BYTES' STEAM challenge.

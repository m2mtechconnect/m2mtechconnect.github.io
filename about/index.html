ABOUT US

Our goal and vision is to be the trusted partner of STEM and Entrepreneurship related hands-on workshops’ content creation  - thereby educate, empower and counter misinformation for our core audience [students (school & university), organizations (small business) and individuals].

We harness the perception, recognition and recommendation abilities of AI and Deep Learning to tailor the learning process for our core audience.

We specialize in Edtech, FinTech, Technology Meetups and Creative Arts. Tech Bytes is our not-for-profit Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Math (STEAM) related Challenges and Competitions initiative for youths, by youths!
